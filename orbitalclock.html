星光飛秒鐘-七言律詩
· 押下平七陽韻 ·
三環星軌耀天光， 時緩分飛秒吐芒。 秒針剎那無停息， 分絲輕曳越穹蒼。 時軌悠旋移歲暮， 我心此刻映四方。 宇宙無言星有跡， 你與星輝共此航。
韻腳：光 · 芒 · 蒼 · 方 · 航　｜　仄收：暮（去）· 息（入）· 跡（入）
Three orbital rings blaze with light,
hours drift slow as seconds ignite.
Each fleeting second leaves no trace,
the minute-thread sweeps heavens untold.
The hour-arc turns while years grow old,
My heart is reflected in all space at this moment.
The cosmos silent — stars still mark their way;
you and their glow share the same voyage today.
