人生時鐘七言律詩 · 押東韻 ·
① 人生似鐘日夜東， ◆「東」押韻 Life turns like a clock, ceaselessly eastward through day and night,
② 千轉萬回步履同。 ◆「同」押韻 A thousand revolutions, ten thousand turns — each step its echo finds.
③ 少年輕踏朝霞路， 　　In youth we tread lightly on the path of dawn's rosy glow,
④ 白首徐行暮色中。 ◆「中」押韻 In silver age we walk with care through dusk's fading light.
⑤ 得失榮枯皆是客， 　　Gain and loss, flourish and withering — all are fleeting guests,
⑥ 悲歡離合共乘風。 ◆「風」押韻 Sorrow and joy, parting and reunion — all ride the same wind.
⑦ 莫讓韶光空白過， 　　Let not the golden years slip past in blankness and regret,
⑧ 一寸光陰一寸功。 ◆「功」押韻 Each inch of time is worth an inch of worthy deed.
押東韻（平聲） 東 · 同 · 中 · 風 · 功 偶句入韻，奇句仄收
